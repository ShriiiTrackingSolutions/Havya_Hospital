Expert Gynecology Care at Havya Women's Hospital

Women’s health is a lifelong journey that deserves attention, compassion and expert medical support. At Havya Women's Hospital, our gynecology department is committed to addressing every stage of a woman’s reproductive health — from adolescence through menopause and beyond — with personalized care and advanced medical solutions.

Comprehensive Women’s Health Services

Our gynecology services cover a wide range of conditions and preventive care treatments to ensure your health and wellness:

Menstrual Irregularities

From heavy or painful periods to missed or irregular cycles, we provide thorough evaluations and treatments tailored to hormonal balance and lifestyle.

Natural Family Planning

Learn to track fertility patterns and menstrual cycles for pregnancy prevention or planning, guided by our specialists.

Pelvic Pain and Infections

We diagnose and treat pelvic inflammatory disease (PID), urinary tract infections (UTIs), vaginal infections and other causes of chronic discomfort.

Hormonal Imbalances

Conditions like Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), thyroid disorders and early menopause are managed through customized treatment plans.

Reproductive Health Screenings

Routine Pap smears, breast exams and HPV testing are essential to early detection and peace of mind.

Specialized Care for All Ages

Gynecological care is essential at every life stage. Our approach changes with your body’s evolving needs:

Adolescent Gynecology

Early guidance in menstrual health, hygiene and emotional support for teenage girls.

Pre-Conception and Fertility Support

Pre-pregnancy checkups to assess reproductive readiness and resolve underlying issues.

Perimenopause and Menopause Management

Hormonal therapy, lifestyle adjustments and support for hot flashes, mood changes, osteoporosis and cervical cancer screening.

Minimally Invasive Procedures and Advanced Diagnostics

Havya Women's Hospital uses state-of-the-art technology for diagnosis and surgical treatments:

Ultrasound and Imaging

High-resolution scans for uterus, ovaries and pelvic structures.

Hysteroscopy

Safe, minimally invasive techniques for diagnosing and treating uterine abnormalities.

Day-Care Surgeries

Outpatient procedures like D&C, polypectomy, Sterilization and biopsy are performed with maximum care and minimal recovery time.

A Safe Space for Every Woman

We believe gynecological care should be free of fear and stigma. Whether it’s your first consultation or you’re dealing with a long-standing issue, our doctors prioritize comfort, dignity and trust.

Your Health. Your Voice. Your Care.

At Havya Women's Hospital, you are involved in every decision about your care. We provide transparent communication, evidence-based treatments and respectful support throughout your wellness journey.

Prioritize Your Well-Being Today

Routine gynecological visits are key to lifelong health. Don’t wait for symptoms to appear — book your appointment at Havya Women's Hospital and take proactive steps toward complete women’s wellness.
