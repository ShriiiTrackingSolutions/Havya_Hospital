What Makes Havya a Trusted Choice for Women

Infertility

  • We offer personalized infertility treatment plans tailored to each couple’s medical profile and reproductive goals.
  • High-precision fertility diagnostics and hormone-level monitoring to identify underlying causes efficiently.
  • Comprehensive IUI, IVF, and hormonal therapy services conducted with the latest assisted reproductive technology.
  • Compassionate emotional support and counseling to help couples navigate every stage of their fertility journey.

Gynecology

  • Expert care for menstrual irregularities, PCOS, fibroids, and hormonal imbalances in women of all ages.
  • Comprehensive screenings including Pap smears, breast exams, and pelvic ultrasounds for early detection.
  • Treatment for infections, pelvic pain, and reproductive system conditions with a focus on long-term health.
  • Customized gynecological guidance from adolescence through menopause with privacy and dignity.

Family Planning

  • Confidential counseling and personalized guidance to help couples make informed family planning decisions.
  • Wide range of contraceptive options including oral pills, IUDs, implants, and permanent solutions tailored to individual needs.
  • Preconception planning services to support healthy and timely pregnancies when the couple is ready.
  • Postpartum contraception and spacing methods to ensure maternal health and child well-being.

Maternity

  • Comprehensive antenatal care with regular monitoring, screenings, and personalized pregnancy guidance.
  • Management of high-risk pregnancies with advanced diagnostic tools and continuous specialist oversight.
  • Comfortable labor rooms and facilities for both natural and C-section deliveries with expert obstetricians.
  • Postnatal support including breastfeeding counseling, maternal health monitoring, and newborn care education.
