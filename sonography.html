What is Sonography?

Sonography is a safe, non-invasive imaging technique that uses high-frequency sound waves to create real-time images of the body’s internal organs and soft tissues. Unlike X-rays, it does not involve radiation, making it ideal for use in pregnancy and reproductive health.

At Havya Women Hospital, our certified doctors utilize the latest ultrasound machines to deliver accurate, clear, and quick results that assist in both diagnosis and treatment planning.

What is Sonography?

Our specialists may recommend a sonography scan for several important reasons, including:

  • Pregnancy detection & monitoring: To track fetal development, heartbeat, and well-being during various stages of pregnancy.
  • Follicular Study: To monitor ovulation cycles for patients undergoing fertility treatment.
  • Pelvic Ultrasound: To examine the uterus, ovaries, fallopian tubes, and bladder for any abnormalities or infections.
  • Abdominal Scan: To detect conditions related to kidneys, liver, gallbladder, and other abdominal organs.
  • Breast Ultrasound: To assess lumps or abnormalities in breast tissue.
  • Transvaginal Ultrasound: For a closer and more detailed view of reproductive organs.
  • Gynecological Disorders: Diagnosing uterine fibroids, ovarian cysts, endometriosis, and polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS).

What to Expect During Your Scan

Most ultrasound procedures are quick and do not require any special preparation. During the scan, a cool gel will be applied to your skin, and a small probe (transducer) will be moved across the area to capture internal images.

Our team will guide you through the process, ensuring privacy, comfort, and a clear understanding of your results.
